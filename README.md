## Project Overview
This project focuses on building an interactive dashboard to monitor **sales performance, profitability, and customer behavior** in a structured and scalable way.

As a **BI Analyst**, I was responsible for translating business requirements from the management team into a comprehensive dashboard that supports fast, data-driven decision-making.

## Goals
- Analyze overall business performance (Sales, Profit, Quantity Sold)
- Identify trends in sales, profit, and orders over time
- Evaluate product performance by sub-category
- Monitor weekly sales and profit trends
- Analyze customer growth and engagement
- Understand customer purchasing behavior
- Identify top customers based on profit contribution

## Key Dashboard
